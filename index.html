History & Mission

Lahontan Paramedical has been serving Yerington and Lyon County for the past 25 years. Founded by Pamela Hunewill, the business has built a long-standing reputation as a family-owned and operated pillar of the local community. We are excited to continue this legacy under the new ownership of the Warren family.

Our Mission

At Lahontan Paramedical, our mission is to provide comprehensive DOT and Non-DOT drug and alcohol testing for our local community and the surrounding area. As a proud member of NDASA, we also offer CDL physicals by appointment to ensure all your regulatory needs are met in one place. We maintain partnerships with major laboratories including Quest, Labcorp, Medtox, CRL, Omega, Alere, Abbott, and Escreen to guarantee reliable results. Our dedicated team strives to provide professional, streamlined service to every client we serve.
